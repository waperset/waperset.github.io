How to make neon wallpaper

  1. Love neon lights? Bring the look into your home with neon wallpaper!

  2. Neon wallpaper is a great way to add some fun and personality to your walls.

  4. Start by finding a design or image that you love. You can find plenty of options online or in magazines.

  6. Cut out the design, then peel off the backing and stick it onto your wall.
