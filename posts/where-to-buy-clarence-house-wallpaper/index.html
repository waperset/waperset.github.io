Background: Origins of Kawaii Culture Kawaii culture originated in Japan, specifically in the cities of Tokyo and Osaka. These characteristics arose from a desire among Japanese youth to look cute and stylish. The term “kawaii” was first used in the 1970s to describe Japanese comic books and animation that were aimed at children.
