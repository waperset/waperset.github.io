How to hang beach wallpaper Beach wallpapers are a great way to bring the outdoors in and add a touch of summer to your home décor. Here are a few tips on how to hang beach wallpaper:

  1. Choose a wall that gets plenty of light for your beach wallpaper. This will help to brighten up the room and create a cheerful atmosphere.

  2. Make sure the surface you’re going to be wallpapering is clean, dry and smooth. Any bumps or unevenness will show through the wallpaper.

  3. When measuring for your beach wallpaper, add an extra inch or two around all sides so you have some wiggle room when hanging it.

  4. Apply paste or adhesive to the back of the wallpaper according to the manufacturer’s instructions. Be generous with the amount you use so the paper will stick well to the wall.
